PNG NID Data Privacy and Consent Agreement

By proceeding with your National ID registration, you consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your personal data by the Papua New Guinea Civil & Identity Registry (PNGCIR) for the following purposes:

  1. Identity Verification: Your data will be used to verify your identity and establish your official record in the national database.
  2. Service Delivery: Your information will facilitate access to government services, voting, banking, and other essential services.
  3. Security Purposes: Data may be used for national security, fraud prevention, and law enforcement purposes as authorized by law.
  4. Statistical Analysis: Anonymous, aggregated data may be used for statistical reporting and national planning.

Data Protection

PNGCIR is committed to protecting your personal information:

  • All data is stored securely with encryption and access controls
  • Your information will only be shared with authorized entities as permitted by law
  • You have the right to access and correct your personal information
  • Data will be retained in accordance with PNG data protection regulations

By consenting below, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy notice and agree to the collection and use of your personal data as described.

PNG Civil & Identity Registry

The National Identification (NID) project is a key initiative of the Government of Papua New Guinea to establish a comprehensive civil registry and identity system for all citizens. This system will enable better access to services and participation in the digital economy.
